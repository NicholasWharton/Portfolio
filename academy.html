STEAM is an acronym that stands for science, technology, engineering, art, and mathematics. This is one of the main principles that the Academy focuses on. Project based Interdisciplinary learning is the other main principle that is followed. This means that all of our learning is done through projects where all of our subjects are pulled in to create one cohesive final deliverable.
