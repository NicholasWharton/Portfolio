Idle Game

This idle game was created in the Adopt a Cause project in the Plano Academy High School. This game was made to represent the how a natural disaster relief organization runs their operations. This can be seen through the receiving of donations, and the buying of utilities to help fight disasters that appear over time. The majority of the icons that are used for the buttons were created by me using photoshop.
